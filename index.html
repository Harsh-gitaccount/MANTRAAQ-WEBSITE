About Us

From Bihar's Waters
to Your Wellness.

Mantraaq is a Begusarai-born agritech startup transforming India's forgotten superfood water chestnut (singhara) into premium, clean-label nutrition. We combine cold-processing technology with QR traceability to deliver gluten-free, diabetic-friendly products that keep farmers thriving and consumers healthy.

üåæ
The Bihar Advantage

Bihar produces 42% of India's singhara, yet farmers face low incomes due to rapid spoilage and zero branding. We're changing that partnering directly with growers across 3 districts and leveraging innovations from the Makhana Research Center to build a supply chain that rewards quality and sustains livelihoods.
