Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 02 June 2026

MantraAQ ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or place an order.

1 Information We Collect

When you interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address & PIN code
  • Order history
  • Payment-related metadata (we do not store card or UPI details)
  • Device and browser information (via cookies/analytics)

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Communicate order updates via SMS, WhatsApp, or email
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Send promotional content (only with your consent)
  • Comply with legal requirements

3 Payment Security

  • All online payments are processed through PayU, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway.
  • We never store your credit/debit card details, UPI ID, or net banking credentials.

4 Sharing of Information

We may share your data with:

  • Logistics partners (for delivery)
  • Payment gateways (for transactions)
  • Analytics platforms (for website improvement)

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

5 Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyse website traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

6 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

7 Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for promotional communication

To exercise your rights, email us at hello@mantraaq.com.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with the revised date.
