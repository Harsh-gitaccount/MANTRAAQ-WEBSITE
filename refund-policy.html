Refund & Replacement Policy

Last Updated: 02 June 2026

🛡️ Our Commitment

At MantraAQ, customer satisfaction is our top priority. Since we deal with food products, please read this policy carefully.

✅ Eligible Cases for Refund or Replacement

We accept refund or replacement requests only in the following cases:

  • Product received in damaged condition
  • Wrong product delivered
  • Product quality issue (e.g., spoiled, tampered packaging)
Note: We do not accept returns for change of mind, taste preference, or opened/consumed products.

📋 How to Report an Issue

  • Contact us within 48 hours of receiving the product.
  • Share the following:
    • Order ID
    • Clear photos/videos showing the issue
    • Description of the problem

You can raise a request via:

🔍 Review & Resolution

  • Once we receive your complaint, we will review the evidence and respond within 3–5 business days.
  • If the complaint is valid:
    • A replacement will be shipped at no extra cost, OR
    • A full refund will be processed to the original payment method.

💳 Refund Processing

  • Refund to original payment method: 5–10 business days
  • Refund timeline may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

🚫 Non-Eligible Cases

Refund or replacement will not be applicable if:

  • The product has been opened, used, or consumed
  • The issue is reported after 48 hours of delivery
  • The product damage was caused by the customer
